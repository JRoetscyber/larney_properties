Step into a home where space, character, and opportunity come together in the heart of Nigel Central.

From the moment you arrive, you are welcomed by a sense of solid craftsmanship and warmth. Inside, the home tells a story of years gone by, with its original wooden floors adding a timeless charm that is becoming increasingly rare to find.

The living areas offer generous space for family life to unfold naturally. Whether it’s relaxed evenings, weekend gatherings, or quiet moments at home, this is a space where memories are waiting to be made.

The kitchen and bathrooms remain in their original condition, offering the perfect opportunity for you to design and create a modern space that reflects your own style. For the right buyer, this is not just a house, it’s a canvas.

Set on a large stand, the property opens up a world of possibilities. Children can play freely, pets have room to roam, and there is still space to expand, add, or enhance.

A valuable bonus is the working borehole, helping you maintain your garden and reduce water costs, a practical feature that adds long-term value.

The tandem garage provides secure parking and additional space for storage or hobbies.

This home is ideal for:

A family looking to create their dream home

A buyer who appreciates character and solid structure

An investor or renovator seeking potential and value

Come and explore the potential, your next chapter could start here.
